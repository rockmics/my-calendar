6) Key Takeaways

  • Keep $45K–$50K ultra‑safe for home purchase using TIPS, T‑Bills, and (optionally) I Bonds.
  • Invest $35K in diversified, inflation‑resistant growth ETFs with global and real‑asset exposure.
  • Maintain 10–15% in hard assets (gold, crypto, commodities) as a de‑dollarization hedge.
  • Continue contributing to 401(k) and Roth IRA; rebalance annually.
  • Adjust hedge ratios if inflation or USD trend changes materially.
