Strength: Dumbbell ↔ Bodyweight Substitutions

Swap → Inverted table row or band row · Squeeze shoulder blades

Swap → Push-ups (incline/elevated for joint comfort)

Swap → Pike push-ups or band press

Swap → Hip hinge or single‑leg RDL (BW) · Flat back
